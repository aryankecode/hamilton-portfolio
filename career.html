Career Highlights

Explore Lewis Hamilton's journey to becoming a Formula 1 legend. From his early days in karting to becoming a multiple-time World Champion, Lewis Hamilton has redefined what it means to be an F1 driver.

2007 - F1 Debut

Lewis Hamilton made his Formula 1 debut with McLaren, finishing third in his first race and becoming the first black driver in the sport.

2008 - First World Championship

In only his second season, Hamilton won his first World Championship, becoming the youngest ever Formula 1 World Champion at that time.

2014 - Mercedes Era Begins

Joining Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team marked the beginning of a new era of dominance, with Hamilton winning multiple championships.

2020 - 7th World Championship

Hamilton equaled Michael Schumacher's record of seven World Championships, solidifying his place as one of the greatest F1 drivers of all time.
