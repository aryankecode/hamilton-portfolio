Activism & Philanthropy

Lewis Hamilton is not just a racing legend, but also a champion for social and environmental causes. His commitment to creating a positive impact on and off the track is evident through his various initiatives.

Equality & Diversity

Lewis Hamilton is a vocal advocate for diversity and inclusion, particularly in motorsports. He has established the Hamilton Commission to increase diversity in British motorsport.

Environmental Sustainability

Hamilton is passionate about reducing his carbon footprint. He has adopted a vegan lifestyle and has called for greater environmental responsibility within Formula 1 and beyond.

Philanthropy

Through his foundation, Hamilton supports a wide range of charitable causes, including education for disadvantaged youth and global health initiatives.
